DCB Savings Account

A savings account is a foundational tool in personal finance, designed to encourage every individual to deposit funds, earn interest, and manage daily financial needs efficiently. Whether you are planning for opening a savings account for the first time or even looking to maximise its benefits, understanding the key features, benefits, and types of savings accounts can help making informed decisions.

Due to the growing need for accessible and on-finger banking solutions, a savings account offers not just convenience but also flexibility and steady interest returns. At DCB Bank, multiple savings account options are tailored for different customer needs, offering attractive features and benefits.

Why open a Savings Account?

Whether you're starting the financial journey or diversifying your money management strategy, opening a savings account with DCB Bank offers an easy and efficient way to grow your funds. Features like competitive interest rates, digital banking access, and various account types make it an important financial option.

DCB Savings Account is not just a place to park your money — it's the first step toward disciplined financial management.

Key features of DCB Savings Accounts:

DCB Bank offers a variety of savings account features designed to provide a seamless, convenient, and rewarding banking experience. Let's break them down further:

  • Attractive Interest Rates: We provide superior interest rates, ensuring the funds grow steadily. Whether you're saving for short-term or long-term goals, these competitive savings account interest rates make sure that money works harder for you.
  • Easy Accessibility: We ensure easy access to your account at any time through DCB Internet Banking, DCB Mobile Banking App, DCB Bank on WhatsApp, Debit Cards, and ATMs nationwide. Whether at home, in the office, or traveling, individuals can access their funds and perform various banking operations seamlessly, ensuring that finances are managed on the go.
  • Debit Card: A debit card that is linked to your account can be used for online shopping, ATM withdrawals, and everyday purchases. Some accounts offer exclusive benefits like cashback or reward points, attractive discount offers on travel, dining, hospitality, merchandising, etc.
  • Bill Payments, Tax Payments, and Fund Transfers: Make utility bill payments for services like electricity, gas, and water bills, Direct Tax Payments, and Indirect Tax Payments using UPI, IMPS, and NEFT, making it easier to manage finances through DCB Internet Banking or DCB Mobile Banking App. These facilities help ensure that the payment is never missed and money can be transferred quickly without delays, giving you peace of mind.
  • International Usage: If you travel frequently or shop online from international vendors, DCB Debit Cards can be used for international transactions through DCB Internet Banking or DCB Mobile Banking App. The card enables individuals to make secure purchases globally, whether it's shopping, paying for services, or withdrawing money from ATMs abroad. This global accessibility makes the DCB Savings Account even more versatile, offering convenience wherever you are in the world.

 

Benefits of DCB Savings Accounts:

Opening DCB Savings Account doesn't just offer a place to store the money; it comes with several added benefits that go beyond simple savings. Here's a more in-depth look:

  • Higher Earnings: By offering competitive interest rates, we ensure that idle money in the account works harder. It's an ideal way to grow your savings while enjoying the benefits of a high interest savings account.
  • Liquidity: One of the main advantages of having a savings liquidity it offers. Whether you're dealing with an unexpected expense or need to make an urgent payment, funds can be accessed immediately when needed, whether via ATMs, UPI, or digital banking platforms.
  • Goal-Based Savings: Open savings accounts for specific goals like education, travel, or emergency funds. By saving funds, individuals can keep track of the progress toward each goal. This is a smart way to structure the savings plan, ensuring that the person is financially prepared for any significant milestones in life.
  • Insurance Benefits: Some Debit Cards that are linked to your DCB Savings Account come bundled with complimentary insurance covers, which provide added security and protection. These insurance benefits might include accidental death coverage, medical insurance, or life insurance, depending on the Debit Card type.
  • Seamless Digital Experience: Our digital banking platform allows access to banking services from the comfort of your home. Checking account balance, funds transfer, bill payments, and opening an FD — all can be done through DCB Internet Banking and DCB Mobile Banking App.
  • Nomination Facility: The nomination facility allows an account holder to nominate a family member to claim the funds in the bank account in case of an unforeseen event of the account holder. This ensures that his/ her loved ones will have easy access to the funds, safeguarding the hard-earned money.

 

Types of Savings Accounts:

DCB Bank offers various types of Savings Accounts tailored to meet different customer needs.

  • DCB Special

    • Access to a comprehensive suite of benefits and services designed to meet your personal banking requirements with DCB Special. Individuals can open DCB Special Savings Account with cashback benefits, DCB Fixed Deposit which offers superior returns, and DCB Business Saver Current Account with unique features.
    • A combination of accounts with a Total Relationship Value (TRV) of ₹5 Lakh to be maintained under the DCB Special Program.
    • Cashback: Earn cashback up to ₹16,500 p.a.* on eligible UPI transactions
    • Features: Up to 50% waiver on locker fees and loan processing fees. Complimentary Debit Card with attractive discount offers and insurance cover benefit.

  • DCB Wow Savings Account

    • A Savings Account specially designed for women with exclusive benefits and rewards, empowering women to achieve their financial goals and making the financial journey rewarding.
    • Cashback: Earn cashback up to ₹16,500 p.a. on eligible UPI transactions
    • Features: Exclusive WOW RuPay Platinum Debit Card with exciting perks. Complimentary zero balance Savings Account for a minor child and 50% discount on locker fees.

  • DCB Happy Savings Account

    • Minimum Average Quarterly Balance (AQB): The minimum Average Monthly Balance (AMB) to be maintained in the DCB Happy Savings Account is ₹10,000, and to be eligible for cashback, the AQB requirement is ₹25,000.
    • Cashback: Earn cashback up to ₹7,500 p.a. on eligible UPI transactions
    • Features: High interest, UPI cashback, quarterly credit of rewards and ease of locker facility for your valuables.

  • DCB Elite Savings Account

    • Features: Premium banking with personalized services, exclusive features, and the ability to choose a preferred 8-digit account number
    • Locker: Enjoy a 50% discount on locker rentals, subject to the maintenance of an average quarterly balance of ₹ 1 Lakh in the account.

  • DCB Niyo Savings Account

    • Zero Foreign Mark-up: Zero foreign exchange mark-up on all your international debit card transactions.
    • Features: Zero FOREX mark-up, attractive interest rates, no joining fee, no annual maintenance charges, and cashback up to ₹7,500 p.a. on eligible UPI transactions and AQB of ₹25,000

  • DCB SmartCash Savings Account

    • Minimum Average Quarterly Balance (AQB): The minimum Average Monthly Balance (AMB) to be maintained in DCB SmartCash Savings Account is ₹10,000, and to be eligible for cashback, the AQB requirement is ₹50,000.
    • Cashback: Earn cashback up to ₹9,600 p.a. on transactions made using the linked Debit Card
    • Features: Attractive interest rate, free internet and mobile banking, and a Debit Card with a choice of Visa or Rupay Platinum Card.

  • DCB Privilege Savings Account

    • Minimum Total Relationship Value (TRV): ₹1 Lakh across Savings Account, Current Account, and Fixed Deposit
    • Features: Free internet and mobile banking, free demand drafts and pay orders up to ₹5 Crore per month, and priority service at branches for expedited banking transactions

  • DCB Classic Savings Account

    • Minimum Average Monthly Balance (AMB): ₹5,000 (Tier 1 branches); ₹2,500 (Other branches)
    • Features: Free internet and mobile banking, free access to DCB Bank ATMs, Debit Card, and attractive interest on account balance

  • DCB Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account

    • Minimum Average Monthly Balance (AMB): Nil
    • Features: Zero balance account with free ATM card, free personal internet banking, and basic banking services

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the interest rate on DCB Savings Account?

DCB Bank offers attractive savings account interest rates depending on your account balance. Please check the Savings Account Interest Rates for more details on different slabs.

How is interest calculated on DCB Savings Account?

Interest is calculated daily based on the account’s closing balance and is credited quarterly. You can also manually calculate interest on a savings account using the formula.

Is there a minimum balance requirement?

Yes, depending on the type of savings account.

How can I open a DCB Savings Account?

Opening a DCB Savings Account is simple and convenient. Visit the nearest DCB Bank branch or open digitally via DCB Zippi Online Fixed Deposit website/ DCB Zippi+ Mobile App (available on both Google Play Store and Apple App Store), with the required documents, including proof of identity, address, and photographs. Alternatively, you can call DCB Customer Care at 02268997777 or 040 68157777 or click here to apply now and submit the required details.

What documents are required to open DCB Savings Account?

You will need a valid identity proof, address proof, PAN card, and passport-size photographs.

How often is interest credited to a DCB Savings Account?

Interest is credited quarterly.

Can I link my DCB Savings Account to investments?

Yes, you can link it to mutual funds, fixed deposits, and recurring deposits for easier fund management.

What happens if I don’t maintain the minimum balance?

A non-maintenance charge is levied as per DCB Bank’s policy. It's advisable to maintain the prescribed minimum average balance.

Can minors open a DCB Savings Account?

Yes, minors can open a savings account under the guardianship of a parent or guardian.

Is the interest earned taxable?

Yes, the interest earned is taxable under the Income Tax Act rules. However, up to ₹10,000 earned in interest in a year is exempt under Section 80TTA.
